Air Fryer — Chicken (lunch)

  • 200g breast, season both sides
  • Salt + pepper + paprika
  • 180°C / 360°F — 18 min
  • Flip once at 10 min
  • Rest 3 min, then slice

Air Fryer — Beef (dinner)

  • 200g colita de cuadril
  • Salt + pepper + rosemary
  • 200°C / 400°F — 12 min (medium)
  • Flip once at 6 min
  • Rest 5 min, then slice

Air Fryer — Potatoes (dinner)

  • 200g potato, cut into 2 cm cubes
  • Season: salt + paprika
  • 200°C / 400°F — 18 min
  • Shake basket at 10 min
  • Golden and crispy = done

Air Fryer — French Toast

  • Mix egg + white + whey + milk
  • Dip 3 bread slices in batter
  • 180°C / 360°F — 8 min
  • Flip once at 4 min
  • Top with fruit + PB

Batch Cooking Shortcut

  • Air fry 4 chicken breasts (lunch) + 4 beef steaks + dinner potatoes on Sunday and Wednesday. Reheat in 3 min.
  • French toast is best made fresh (3 min prep + 8 min air fryer = 11 min total). The batter takes 30 seconds.
